About Us

Your Health, Our Priority — Compassionate Care Anytime, Anywhere

At PulsePro Clinic, we are redefining healthcare with a focus on dedication, accessibility, and quality. Based in Whitby, Ontario, our nurse practitioner-led virtual family practice proudly serves the community. We are committed to providing personalized, compassionate, and comprehensive care to individuals and families across Ontario—whether through virtual consultations, phone visits, or in-home care.

Our innovative telehealth solutions break down barriers to access, offering flexible after-hours and weekend availability to fit your busy life. At PulsePro Clinic, healthcare is a trusted partnership—one that places your well-being at the center of everything we do.

We welcome everyone, including individuals without OHIP coverage, and are proud to serve our community with inclusivity and respect—free from discrimination.

Our Philosophy of Care

At PulsePro Clinic, we believe healthcare should be accessible, compassionate, and tailored to each individual. Our goal is to break down barriers to care through flexible, innovative solutions—supporting your health journey with empathy and expertise. We welcome all individuals, regardless of background or coverage, and are committed to helping our community thrive.

Meet Radha Venka Reddy – The Heart Behind PulsePro Clinic

Ms. Radha Venka Reddy is a highly skilled Family Nurse Practitioner with a Master of Science in Nursing (MScN) and a Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BScN). With a strong foundation in holistic and patient-centered care, she is dedicated to delivering high-quality, accessible healthcare to individuals and families across Ontario.

Radha brings extensive experience in primary, elderly, and palliative care, with a compassionate and collaborative approach to health and wellness. As a nurse practitioner, she is deeply committed to promoting patient dignity, trust, and continuity of care. Her clinical practice focuses on prevention, early intervention, and chronic disease management, tailored to the unique needs of each individual.

She plays a key leadership role at PulsePro Clinic, a nurse practitioner-led virtual family practice based in Whitby, Ontario. Passionate about removing barriers to care, Radha champions innovative telehealth solutions, ensuring that quality healthcare is just a call or click away—even for those without OHIP coverage.

With fluency in four languages and a culturally sensitive approach, she serves a diverse population with empathy, respect, and professionalism. Radha’s mission is to empower patients on their healthcare journey and foster a trusted environment where everyone feels seen, heard, and cared for.

Virtual Care Clinic Disclaimer

The services provided by PulsePro Clinic through virtual care are for general medical consultations, follow-ups, and non-emergency healthcare needs. By using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

Not for Emergencies

Virtual consultations are not a substitute for emergency care. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or visit the nearest emergency room.

Technology Limitations

While we strive for high-quality virtual care, technical issues such as poor internet connections may impact service quality.

Accuracy of Information

The effectiveness of virtual consultations depends on accurate and complete information from the patient. Misrepresentation may impact care quality.

Privacy & Security

We comply with strict privacy policies, but virtual communication carries inherent risks, such as unauthorized access or data breaches.

Medical Advice & Prescriptions

Any diagnosis, treatment plan, or prescription provided is based on available information. In-person follow-ups or lab tests may be required.

Geographic Restrictions

Virtual care services are subject to provincial regulations. Patients must confirm eligibility based on their location.

By proceeding with a virtual consultation, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted this disclaimer. For any questions or concerns, please contact PulsePro Clinic prior to your appointment.
