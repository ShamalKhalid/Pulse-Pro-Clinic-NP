What is Virtual Care?

“Consultation using your computer or phone"

We can call, videochat, fax, send questionnaires, and securely email.

We are happy to answer any questions you have. If we are unable to help, we will do our best to get you to the right person and/or place.

How does this work?

  • Click the "Book a Virtual Visit" Button
  • Choose the Type of Appointment You Would Like to Have
  • Paying For Your Appointment

At the time of your visit, you will be linked to a secure electronic medical system powered by Juvonno EMR Software.

How Does It Work

Click the "Book a Virtual Visit " Button

This link will take you to the secure website for you to register as a patient and book a health care appointment.

Book a Virtual Visit
Choose the Type of Appointment You Would Like to Have

You can book virtual visits (video or phone) and in-person visits for medical issues. Identify who the visit is for, select the reason, date/time, and sign up to create your account.

Paying For Your Appointment

You will not have to pay until you have finished your appointment. We will send you an invoice and link for payment.

Answer the Medical Questions We Send to You

We want to hear your story. Tell us in your own words what’s going on.

Notifications and Reminders

We’ll send you email and text messages with the link to open the video conference. We’ll also remind you 15 minutes before your appointment.

Your Appointment

Click the secure link that was sent to you by email and login at the right time. We will do our best to answer your questions or refer you to the right provider.
